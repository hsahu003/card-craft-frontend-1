# Using the Multi-Select Feature

The multi-select feature allows you to group multiple text elements and stickers together so you can move, duplicate, delete, or resize them all at once. It is designed to work seamlessly whether you are designing on a desktop computer or a mobile device.

## Selecting Multiple Elements

There are a few different ways to select multiple items depending on the device you are using:

### On Mobile Devices
1. **Tap the First Item:** Tap on any text or sticker to select it. The element control bar will appear at the bottom of the screen.
2. **Turn on Multi Select:** Tap the **Multi Select** button in the control bar. The button will turn blue to indicate that the multi-select mode is active.
3. **Tap to Add:** While the button is blue, simply tap any other text elements or stickers on your canvas to add them to your selection. 
4. **Tap to Remove:** If you accidentally select an item, just tap it again while the mode is active to remove it from your selection.

### On Desktop Computers
1. **Keyboard Shortcuts:** Hold down the `Shift` key (or `Ctrl`/`Cmd`) and click on the elements you want to group together.
2. **Marquee Selection:** Click and drag your mouse starting from an empty area on the canvas to draw a dotted blue selection box. Anything inside the box will be instantly selected.

---

## Interacting with Your Selection

Once you have multiple items selected, a unified bounding box will appear around the entire group.

* **Moving:** To move the group on mobile, press and drag the blue drag handle that appears beneath the group. On desktop, simply click and drag anywhere inside the bounding box.
* **Resizing:** Drag the corner handles of the unified bounding box to scale all elements in the group together.
* **Actions:** Use the buttons in the control bar to **Duplicate** or **Delete** the entire group simultaneously.

---

## Smart Automation

To keep your workflow fast and prevent accidental deselections, the editor will automatically manage the Multi Select toggle for you:

* **Automatic Drag Detection:** If you forget to turn off the Multi Select toggle and attempt to move the group by dragging the handles, the editor will instantly turn the toggle off. This ensures your items are moved safely instead of being deselected!
* **Easy Deselection:** Tapping anywhere on the empty canvas background will instantly clear your selection and turn off the Multi Select toggle.
* **Opening Menus:** If you click the **Stickers** menu to add a new item, the Multi Select mode is automatically turned off to prepare for your new sticker.
